EEG Signal Acquisition & BCI Analysis

EEG Analyst and BCI Prototyper | 14-channel EEG, signal processing, neurofeedback

Overview

I calibrated a 14-channel EEG system, captured clean signals under varied states, and built pipelines for band analysis, artifact removal, and real-time BCI demos.

Tools

  • EEG headset with 14 electrodes
  • BCI software and custom processing steps

Role

EEG Analyst and BCI Prototyper

Description

I ensured 100 percent contact quality and analyzed Alpha, Beta, Theta, and Gamma bands to estimate relaxation, attention, and stress. I built artifact removal for eye blinks and muscle noise. I trained models to recognize mental commands and facial expressions and demonstrated live neurofeedback. The project gave me practical experience in biosignal calibration and cognitive state decoding for neurotech.
