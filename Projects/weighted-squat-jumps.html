Weighted Squat Jumps Aiding Device Development

Embedded Systems and Mechanical Designer | SolidWorks, 3D printing, microcontrollers

Overview

I prototyped an aiding device to standardize weighted squat jumps for training and rehab with controls for depth and tempo and optional sensor logging.

Tools

  • SolidWorks for CAD and drawings
  • 3D printing for rapid iteration
  • Microcontroller platform for optional sensing
  • Excel for performance logs

Role

Embedded Systems and Mechanical Designer

Description

I modeled the mechanism, built test fixtures, and tuned geometry for safe, repeatable motion. I explored displacement and velocity sensing and created an export to Excel for rep tracking. The design keeps cost low and allows future upgrades such as closed-loop control or wireless dashboards. The goal is to improve training consistency and rehab outcomes through guided mechanics and basic instrumentation.
