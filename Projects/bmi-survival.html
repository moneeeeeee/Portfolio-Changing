BMI-Stratified Survival Analysis

Statistical Analyst | R, survival, survminer, TCGA clinical data

Overview

I examined survival differences across BMI categories in TCGA KIRC clinical data using Kaplan–Meier models with log-rank tests.

Tools

  • R with survival and survminer packages
  • BMI class variable engineered from CDC ranges

Role

Statistical Analyst

Description

I created a categorical bmi.class feature and generated survival curves and risk tables by group. I used log-rank testing for significance and summarized clinical relevance with clear plots. The work reinforced feature engineering, hypothesis-driven analysis, and clean reporting for clinical variables.
