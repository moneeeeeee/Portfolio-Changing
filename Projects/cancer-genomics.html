Cancer Genomics Data Analysis

Data Analyst and Pipeline Developer | R, DESeq2, ggplot2, TCGA

Overview

I helped build a pipeline in R to study mutation patterns and survival in TCGA kidney cancer patients. We clustered by frequent mutations, compared outcomes, and explored pathway changes using RNA expression.

Tools

  • R, tidyverse
  • DESeq2 for RNA-seq analysis
  • ggplot2 for plots
  • TCGA datasets and cBioPortal

Role

Data Analyst and Pipeline Developer

Description

I grouped patients using Ward.D2 hierarchical clustering on binary mutation profiles for the 50 most altered genes. Kaplan–Meier curves and log-rank tests compared survival between clusters. PCA helped confirm subgroup structure and guided our comparisons.

I ran DESeq2 to analyze expression differences and used pathway enrichment to highlight metabolism and immune signaling. I handled preprocessing, normalization, and visualizations including survival curves, heatmaps, and pathway plots. The pipeline is modular and reproducible and improved my skills in unsupervised learning, survival models, and biological interpretation.
