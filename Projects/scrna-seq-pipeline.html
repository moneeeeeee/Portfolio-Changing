Single-Cell RNA-Seq Data Analysis and Alignment Pipeline

Bioinformatics Analyst | R, SAM parsing, custom algorithms

Overview

I implemented a scRNA-seq pipeline with sequence alignment concepts, SAM file parsing, and QC metrics to study mapping quality and coverage.

Tools

  • R for parsing and analysis
  • Custom alignment scoring and traceback simulation
  • ASCII conversion for Phred scores and QC summaries

Role

Bioinformatics Analyst

Description

I simulated pairwise alignment with gap and mismatch penalties, then parsed SAM headers and reads to extract metadata, mapping counts, and mean base quality. I profiled chromosome-specific mappings and examined low-quality regions. I produced boxplots for Phred distributions and flagged reads below thresholds. The work strengthened my understanding of aligners, data formats, and quality control in single-cell workflows.
